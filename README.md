# SparkCoder - ServiceNow Interview Helper 🚀
**Live Demo:** [sparkcoder.online](https://sparkcoder.online)

Welcome to **SparkCoder**, a dedicated interview preparation platform designed specifically for aspiring and transitioning ServiceNow Developers. 

Navigating a ServiceNow technical interview requires more than just knowing the platform—it requires understanding how to apply scripting, business rules, and architecture to real-world business problems. I built SparkCoder to help developers consolidate their knowledge, test their scripting skills, and confidently tackle scenario-based interview questions.

### Key Features
* **Curated Question Bank:** Frequently asked questions covering everything from GlideRecord queries to Client Scripts and ACLs.
* **Scenario Walkthroughs:** Real-world problem-solving exercises mimicking actual technical interview rounds.
* **Scripting Challenges:** Hands-on practice for writing efficient, platform-safe code.
